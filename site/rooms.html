While you are here...

Guest laundry

We provide a self-service Laundromat. Laundry supplies and coin change are available at the front desk.

Microwaves & refrigerators

Rooms are available with a microwave and refrigerator for extended-stay guests. For more information, inquire at the front desk.

Pets

We offer designated rooms specifically for dogs. There is a $5.00 charge per pet per week. A leash is required at all times. Be mindful to have your pet do their business in one of our designated sites and please remove their droppings. NO cats, Pit-bulls, Rotweillers, German Shepards or Dobermans.

Security

Your room is equipped with a safety bolt lock for your security. Please identify visitors through the peephole before letting them in. Do not push lock in door knob when leaving your room, it will lock you out!

Wake-up Service

You can schedule your own wake-up call on our automated system by following the telephone instructions in your room.
